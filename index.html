About Me

About Me

Under the supervision of Hirokazu Shirado, I study the effects of computer-mediated communication on the development of social relationships between diverse people. My work spans the implementation and experimental validation of AI interventions to help these relationships blossom.

My colorful past experiences at University of Maryland, Baltimore County and Kyushu University have filled my life with friendships that I cherish dearly. In my time off, I will travel any distance to reconnect with them.
